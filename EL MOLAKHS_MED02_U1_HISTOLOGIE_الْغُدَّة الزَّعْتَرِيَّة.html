    • 3.1 General organization:
    • The histological structure of the thymus is well apparent in young children.
      • The two thymic lobes have a pyramid shape with a lower base, joined by their internal face.

          • A clearer, central zone, the medullary zone. It contains Hassall's corpuscles.

          • Accessory immune cells presenting antigens.
